The **14th IIIT Advanced Summer School on Natural Language Processing
(IASNLP 2026)** runs at LTRC, IIIT Hyderabad, with this year's edition
focused on **Speech Large Language Models (Speech LLMs)**.

## What to expect

The programme includes a two-day pre-school session to bring everyone up
to a common footing, followed by expert lectures, hands-on sessions, and
guided projects. The summer school series has long been a way for the
centre to open its work up to researchers and students from across the
country and beyond.

## A continuing series

IASNLP has run for over a decade, with recent editions covering themes
such as multimodal NLP and the bridge between speech and text. Focusing
the 2026 edition on Speech LLMs reflects the growing overlap between large
language models and speech technology, an area where the centre's speech
and NLP groups intersect.
