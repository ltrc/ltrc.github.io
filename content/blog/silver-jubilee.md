In December 2024, the Language Technologies Research Centre celebrated
**25 years** since its founding with a two-day event at IIIT Hyderabad.

## A look back, and forward

The celebration brought together talks and panel discussions on the
vision, mission, evolution, and future of language technologies. Over its
first quarter century, LTRC has grown into one of the largest academic
centres for speech and language technology in South Asia, working across
machine translation, natural language processing, speech processing, and
information retrieval.

## Launching BhashaVerse

The Silver Jubilee was also the occasion for one of the centre's largest
open releases to date: BhashaVerse, a collection of translation models
and datasets covering 36 Indian languages. Marking a milestone with an
open contribution to the community reflects how the centre has tended to
approach its work over the years.
